## üîó Key Relationships Summary

### One-to-Many Relationships:
- **restaurants** ‚Üí business_hours, categories, menu_items, customers, orders, staff, tables, etc.
- **categories** ‚Üí menu_items
- **customers** ‚Üí customer_addresses, orders, reviews_ratings, loyalty_transactions
- **orders** ‚Üí order_items, payments, coupon_usage
- **menu_items** ‚Üí menu_item_tags, menu_item_variations, order_items
- **staff** ‚Üí staff_shifts

### Many-to-Many Relationships:
- **menu_items** ‚Üî **addons** (via menu_item_addons)
- **menu_items** ‚Üî **inventory** (via menu_item_ingredients)
- **order_items** ‚Üî **addons** (via order_item_addons)

### Self-Referencing Relationships:
- **customers** can refer to other customers (referral system)
